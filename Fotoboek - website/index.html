Barbara

Because of caring for my daughter, I could not leave Brussels this summer. Then I decided to commit to Cinema Trottoir, a 5 weekend film festival organized by Cinemaximilian asbl. I decided to join the cooking team, led by Ashour. Together we conjured up buffets brimming with goodies from Gaza. Which led to great enjoyment and much conviviality among the numerous visitors.

“I did not miss traveling because CMX is one of those places where the world comes to us“. - Barara

In this way, I was able to experience CMX as a solidary, open place where connections develop between people regardless of their native soil, age, religion, orientation, interests, ... For me personally, it became a necessary place of “focus in the now,” of rolling up your sleeves and being able to be concrete. A breath of fresh air. A warm embrace. A gift in mutual solidarity. Now I look forward to my first winter visits to asylum centers.

Viet

In August, I joined an open lunch at Cinemaximiliaan (CMX) through a friend and immediately felt at home in its warm, collective atmosphere.

“I became an active volunteer, helping with the Cinema Trottoir Festival and Friday cooking sessions, and soon saw CMX as a "family" ”. - Viet

As a filmmaker and immigrant seeking to connect with Belgian society, CMX provided a sense of community I had long been searching for. Currently, CMX supports me as the production entity for my next medium-length documentary, funded by the 2021 Wildcards Award from the Flemish Audio-visual Fonds (VAF). This collaboration highlights CMX's dedication to independent, human-focused documentary cinema, unlike profit-driven production companies. Alongside pursuing a PhD in the arts, I'm committed to nurturing this vital space for artists and immigrants.

Rola

In the past four years, Cinemaximiliaan has transformed my life. We organized three Cinema Trottoir festivals, and I rediscovered my passion as a film critic and discussion leader. Their network opened doors to moderate and critique films, including an invitation from Cinematek Brussels.

Beyond cinema, their programs enriched me personally and artistically. Cinemaximiliaan is more than a platform. It’s a family, a sanctuary of creativity and support.
