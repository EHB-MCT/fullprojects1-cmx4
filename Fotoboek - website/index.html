    Tell Your Story

    Barbara

    Because of caring for my daughter, I could
    not leave Brussels this summer. Then I
    decided to commit to Cinema Trottoir, a 5
    weekend film festival organized by
    Cinemaximilian asbl. I decided to join the
    cooking team, led by Ashour. Together we
    conjured up buffets brimming with goodies
    from Gaza. Which led to great enjoyment
    and much conviviality among the
    numerous visitors.

    “I did not miss traveling because CMX is
    one of those places where the world
    comes to us“. - Barara

    In this way, I was able to experience CMX as
    a solidary, open place where connections
    develop between people regardless of their
    native soil, age, religion, orientation,
    interests, ... For me personally, it became a
    necessary place of “focus in the now,” of
    rolling up your sleeves and being able to be
    concrete. A breath of fresh air. A warm
    embrace. A gift in mutual solidarity. Now I
    look forward to my first winter visits to
    asylum centers.
